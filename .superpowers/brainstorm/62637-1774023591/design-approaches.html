A: Clean Minimal

Closest to shadcn/Vercel. Flat surfaces with subtle borders, 2x2 grid navigation cards, large title typography. No bottom tab bar — pure scroll with sticky header. Neutral palette, content speaks for itself.

Vibe: Vercel dashboard on mobile
Best for: Users who want a clean, no-nonsense tool

B: Editorial List

iOS Settings meets Vercel. Large title with personal greeting, contextual spotlight card (next event/action), then a clean list-style navigation with icons, descriptions, and badges. Chevrons signal "tap to enter." Maximum clarity for older users.

Vibe: Apple Health / iOS Settings, elevated
Best for: Older users who need obvious navigation affordances

C: Bold Cards

Bento-inspired with large touchable cards. Stats row at top, then full-width navigation cards with context. Primary section (Documents) gets an inverted dark card treatment. Bolder, more opinionated — less list, more magazine.

Vibe: Apple Wallet meets Linear
Best for: Making a strong visual impression
