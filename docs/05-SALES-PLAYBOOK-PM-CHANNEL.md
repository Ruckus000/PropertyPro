## STEP 3: 14-DAY PILOT PERIOD

### A. The Micro-Commitment

**Maximum time gap between touchpoints:** 7 days. Two scheduled check-ins divide the pilot into manageable halves.

### B. Pilot Design

|---|---|
| **Most non-compliant** | Biggest before/after improvement to show at review. The compliance score delta is your strongest data point. |
| **Engaged board president** | A friendly board president who will actually log in, provide feedback, and potentially champion the platform to other boards in the PM's portfolio. |

**Do NOT pilot with:** The PM's most difficult board (introduces unnecessary friction), their largest community (too complex), or a community that's already using a competing platform (switching cost muddies the evaluation).
